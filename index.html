OUR VISION

In a world where not a lot of people feel confident, glorious, and luxurious because they are not able to afford it, we believe that everyone should be able to feel like they are, because only then they can achieve this feeling no matter their income.

 

WHAT WE STAND FOR

Affordability

Luxury

Self-esteem

Inclusiveness

Peluche strives to make its products with the best possible quality and at an affordable price. We want that our products are available to everyone who doesn’t have a big budget for cosmetics.

We as Peluche don’t define luxury as expensive. We see luxury as something very fine, comfortable and of good quality no matter its price. We hope to give our consumers this feeling and experience of luxury with our brand.

Peluche strongly believes that everyone should think highly of themselves and walk with confidence in themselves through life. We think you are an amazing and beautiful individual and hope that our products give you that extra confidence because you are worth it!

Every single person should be able to feel luxurious, beautiful and confident. No matter your race, ethnicity, sexual orientation, age or financial situation. We strive to give this feeling to every single person who consumes or is interested in our brand, no matter who you are.
