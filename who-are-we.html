Avely is 21 and from Mustvee in Estonia. She graduated high school in 2020 at her school in Tartu. After she graduated, she moved to the Netherlands for her education. She is now a second-year Creative Business student at BUas. Avely is in the publishing department but loves to try different things in the media field. “I love Peluche because it stands for making sure that everybody can feel confident in their body, and this is something very important to me - that everybody is happy with the way they are.”

Jenna is 20 and from Sint Willebrord in the Netherlands. She graduated from Jan Tinbergen college with her havo diploma in 2019. After this, she started as a student creative business at Inholland in Rotterdam. After passing her propaedeutic year, she had a gap year. She now is a second-year creative business student at BUas in Breda. Jenna is in the publishing department with a passion for writing. “Peluche is an amazing brand because it’s for everyone. The brand is designed for everyone no matter your race, sexual orientation, or financial situation. I love that it’s so inclusive and make all types of women feel confident.”

Lilly is 19 and from Venlo in the Netherlands. She started high school with vwo, where she also did a special art class but ended up getting her havo diploma at Valuascollge in Venlo in 2020. She is now a second-year Creative Business student at BUas, where she is in the AV department. Lilly has a love for design. “I like Peluche because I relate to the vision. I like to spend less on something of good quality and is luxurious.”
